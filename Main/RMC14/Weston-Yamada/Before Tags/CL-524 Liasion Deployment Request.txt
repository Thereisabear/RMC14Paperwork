[head=3]1. Purpose of Deployment[/head]
The Liaison will deploy to the operational area to further Weston-Yamada Corporations operations, protect corporate interests, and/or investigate damage to company property in circumstances that require the presence and/or oversight of the Corporate Liaison. This includes, but is not limited to:
    [bullet]Advertisement and Public Representation
    [bullet]Retrieval of sensitive Corporate information and documentation
    [bullet]Collecting feedback from UNMC personnel
    [bullet]Retrieval of XX-121 Specimens deemed valuable to the Weston-Yamada Corporation  
    [bullet]Assessment of corporate properties and assets
    [bullet]Colonial development and management

For a full list, please contact Weston-Yamada Special Services Division upper management

[head=3]2. Interference of UNMC Operations [/head]
The Liaison will refrain from interfering with any UNMC personnel or operations unless deemed absolutely necessary to ensure Weston-Yamada Corporations core directives are met, as determined by the Liaison.

[head=3]3. Adherence with Military Law and Commissioned Officers [/head]
The Liaison will be required to follow Marine Law and any directives from commissioned officers while within the operational area, unless deemed as necessary to further Weston-Yamada Corporations core directives, as determined by the Liaison. 
Direct Weston-Yamada Corporation directives overwrite any directives from command staff or other personnel, and release the Liaison from Marine Law if needed to complete said directives.
