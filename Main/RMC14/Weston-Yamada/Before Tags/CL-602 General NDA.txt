This [bold]Non-disclosure Agreement[/bold] is made effective as of March 30th 2124 by and between the Weston-Yamada Corporation and the signatory regarding the property and information related to all operational procedures.

[bold]I. Confidential Information:[/bold] The term 'Confidential Information' means any information which is proprietary to Weston-Yamada, whether or not owned or developed by Weston-Yamada, which is generally known other than by Weston-Yamada, which the signatory may obtain through any direct or indirect contact with Weston-Yamada, regardless of whether specifically identified as confidential or proprietary. Confidential Information shall include any information provided by Weston-Yamada concerning the business, technology, and information of Weston-Yamada and any third party with which Weston-Yamada deals, including, without limitation, business records and plans, trade secrets, technical data, product ideas, contracts, financial information, pricing structure, health discounts, computer programs, listings, and unknown wildlife, and all copyright and intellectual property. The nature of the information and the manner of disclosure are such that a reasonable person would understand it is confidential.

[bold]II. Protection of Confidential Information:[/bold] the signatory understands and acknowledges that the Confidential Information has been developed or obtained by Weston-Yamada from investment of significant time, effort, and expense, and that the Confidential Information is valuable, special, and a unique asset of Weston-Yamada which provides Weston-Yamada with significant competitive advantage, and needs to be protected from improper disclosure. [bold]III. Unauthorized Disclosure of Information - Injunction:[/bold] If it appears that the signatory has disclosed (or has threatened to disclose) Confidential Information in violation of this Agreement, Weston-Yamada shall be entitled to an injunction to restrain the signatory from disclosing the Confidential Information in whole or in part. Weston-Yamada shall not be prohibited by this provision from pursuing other remedies, including a claim for losses and damages.

[bold]IV. Non-Circumvention:[/bold] For a period of five hundred (500) years after the end of the terms of this Agreement, the signatory will not attempt to do business with or otherwise solicit any business contacts found or otherwise referred by Weston-Yamada to the signatory for the purpose of circumventing, the result of which shall be to prevent Weston-Yamada from realizing or recognizing a profit, fees, or otherwise, without the specific written approval of Weston-Yamada.
